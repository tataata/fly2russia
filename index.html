Who we are

FLY2RUSSIA is young independent aviation consultancy company.

Our mission is to open Russian and CIS aviation markets for our Partners.

Our small team is a group of experienced aviation professionals.

We know Russian and CIS markets, and we know how to promote and implement products of our Partners in the market.

We have a different consultancy model, our solutions are:

  • Efficient
  • Simple
  • Result-oriented

That is why we offer smart and flexible price policy.
